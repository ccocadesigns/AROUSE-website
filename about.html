Since 2010, AROUSE has served campus as Ohio State University's premier student radio station and cultural magazine. We aim to enrich OSU's moribund cultural life, and do our best to provide an alternative voice to the underappreciated side of the Agricultural and Mechanical College.

If you want to host your own radio show, write articles for AROUSE Magazine, or just get involved, come to our meetings in Enarson 254 every other Tuesday at 7:30 PM. Follow us on Instagram at @arouseosu for more information on meeting dates and times.

AROUSE rejects all kinds of bigotry and harassment. To share any concerns, or to report harassment or discrimination, please reach out to a member of the executive board.
